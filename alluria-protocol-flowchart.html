2. Mint ALUD Stablecoin

Against deposited collateral, users can mint ALUD tokens at a minimum collateralization ratio, maintaining protocol stability

3. Stability Mechanisms

Liquidation system, redemption mechanism, and ALUR incentives work together to maintain ALUD's peg to $1 USD

4. Earn ALUR Rewards

Protocol participants earn ALUR tokens through providing stability pool liquidity, early adopter rewards, and governance participation
